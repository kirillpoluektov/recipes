Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic cloves
  • Red wine (or beef broth)
  • Marinara sauce
  • Dried thyme
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna noodles
  1. Start by making the sauce
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready.
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job.
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
